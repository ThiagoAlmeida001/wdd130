Santiago, Chile

Santiago is the capital of Chile, and it’s right next to the beautiful Andes Mountains. It’s a great city with a mix of cool modern buildings and old historic areas. The people are super friendly, the food is amazing, and there are tons of fun places to explore. Plus, you can visit the mountains or the beach in just a short drive.
